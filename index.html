Why Choose FindIT?

Easy to Use

Simple, intuitive interface designed for quick reporting and claiming of lost items with just a few taps.

Verified College Identity

Only students with valid college emails can report or claim items ensuring trust through real identities.

Photo Verification

Upload photos to help identify and verify lost items quickly.

Secure QR-Based Claiming

Generate and scan a QR code to claim an item securely in person. No false handoffs everything is verified.

Claim Dispute Reporting

If someone falsely claims your item, you can flag it from the claimed list. Admins review and take action fast.

Community Driven

Built by students, for students. Help your college community stay connected and supportive.

How FindIT Works

1

Report

Found something? Report it with details and photos to help others find their belongings.

2

Browse Lost Items

View a list of items that students have reported in real-time. Search by category or location to quickly find matches.

3

Claim with QR

Found your item? Meet the reporter and scan the QR code they generate to securely confirm the handoff.

4

Verified & Tracked

Both parties are logged. If someone falsely claims an item, you can report it from the Claimed Items list.

Privacy Policy

FindIT is committed to protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FindIT app, accessible by students within our college network.

1. Information We Collect
- College Email Verification
- Personal details (Name, Registration Number)
- Lost/Found item reports
- QR Code scans for claim tracking

2. How We Use Your Information
- Authenticate your identity
- Secure item reports and claims
- Maintain transaction logs
- Improve app performance and support

3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your information. Limited data is visible to other users involved in claims for verification purposes only.

4. Data Security
We use secure authentication, encryption, and access restrictions to protect your data.

5. Retention
We retain data only while your account is active or as required by institutional policy.

6. Your Rights
- Access, update or delete your reported item
- Report false claims
- Request support

7. Policy Updates
Any changes to this policy will be posted and announced via the app.
